About Lucid

Lucid is Southampton’s original headshop and alternative lifestyle store, proudly independent for over 25 years.

We offer one of the city’s widest ranges of smoking accessories — from rolling papers, filters, blunt wraps, cones, lighters, grinders, bongs, pipes and trays to RAW lifestyle gear and stash products. Our shelves also feature CBD products, vape hardware and e-liquids, IQOS Devices and the full range of TEREA sticks, as well as the full range of ZYN nicotine pouches.

Beyond smoking and vaping, we carry Southampton’s biggest selection of piercing jewellery, plus Cuban cigars, hair dye, festival accessories and seasonal items. At Lucid we pride ourselves on genuine products, competitive prices, and friendly, non‑judgemental service for students, professionals, ravers and the city’s alternative community.
